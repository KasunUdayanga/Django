About Us

Based in colombo, Illinois, Little Lemon is a family owned Mediterranean restaurant, focused on traditional recipes served with a modern twist. The chefs draw inspiration from Italian, Greek, and Turkish culture and have a menu of 12-15 items that they rotate seasonally. The restaurant has a rustic and relaxed atmosphere with moderate prices, making it a popular place for a meal any time of the day. Little Lemon is owned by two Italian brothers, Kasun and Bcc, who moved to the United States to pursue their shared dream of owning a restaurant.
